05 — What To Expect

What fixing this actually unlocks.

50–100x
Organic traffic growth, 12 months
  • Starting from just 1 monthly visit today, even 50–100 monthly visits is a 50–100x jump
  • Still below every competitor benchmarked here (41–344 monthly visits) — a realistic, not inflated, target
60–120+
Realistic keyword footprint target
  • Direct competitors already rank for 36–124 keywords with far less market coverage than your seven Ontario locations
  • Every media kit becomes its own keyword opportunity once it has real content behind it
188/mo
Leads currently going to competitors
  • Top Outdoor pulls 189 monthly search visits to Eye Candy Ads' 1 — same market, same medium, same opportunity
  • Even a small share of that converting to inquiries is real pipeline sitting on the table today
Up to 24%
Lower bounce rate from Core Web Vitals fixes
  • Google's own research links passing Core Web Vitals to meaningfully lower bounce rates and higher mobile conversion
  • Fixing the CLS and LCP issues flagged in the audit is the fastest lever available
Ranges reflect typical outcomes for comparable local-service & OOH sites and published Core Web Vitals research — not a guarantee.

Lead capture & automation

  • One inbox for every inquiry — web, phone, and ad clicks

  • Reporting that shows what's working, month over month
